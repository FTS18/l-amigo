System Features

Everything you need to stay motivated and compete with your friends.

Stay Connected

Keep up with your friends' live stats, streaks, and velocity metrics all in one place.

Values Comparison

Side-by-side comparison with precision charts for difficulty, topics, and languages.

Easy Import/Export

Share your friend list seamlessly across multiple devices and browsers with one click.

Activity Feed

Real-time "Recent Solves" detection. Know exactly what your peers are tackling.

365 Days History

Full year of submission history. Accurate streaks, trends, and activity heatmaps.

Predict Standings

See Codeforces rating changes and college standings directly injected into the platform natively.

Live Standings Prediction

We inject a predictive rating algorithm directly into Codeforces during live contests. Instantly see your expected Elo delta dynamically rendered in the standings table without waiting for system testing to finish.

View Technical Details

Automated GitHub Sync

Never manually push code again. L'Amigo automatically syncs your accepted LeetCode and Codeforces solutions to a structured GitHub repository. It even generates an auto-updating README and stats badge.

Setup Git Sync

Unified Dashboard & Activity Feed

Track your friends' progress across LeetCode, Codeforces, and CodeChef in one centralized dashboard. See exactly who solved what problem in real-time, giving you the motivation to keep your streak alive.

Deep Analytics & Insights

Analyze your problem-solving patterns with beautiful charts. View breakdowns by difficulty, topics, and languages to understand your strengths and weaknesses compared to your peers.

Head-to-Head Comparison

Go head-to-head with any friend. Compare your total solves, active streaks, and recent activity. Our algorithm even suggests problems your friend has solved that you haven't, giving you a curated list of challenges to tackle.
