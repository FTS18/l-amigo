System Features

Everything you need to track and compete with your friends.

Track 50 Friends

Monitor up to 50 friends with live stats, streaks, and velocity metrics all in one place.

Values Comparison

Side-by-side comparison with precision charts for difficulty, topics, and languages.

Internal GitHub Sync

Auto-push your accepted solutions to GitHub, organized by difficulty level.

Auto Detection

Real-time "Accepted" verdict detection. Solutions sync instantly to your repo.

365 Days History

Full year of submission history. Accurate streaks, trends, and activity insights.

Discover Problems

Find problems your friends solved that you haven't. Never miss a good challenge.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about L'Amigo and how it works.

Is L'Amigo free?

100% free and open-source. No premium features, ads, or tracking. Built by developers for developers.

Data Privacy

Local storage only. Nothing is sent to third-party servers except direct API calls to platform providers.

Friend Tracking

Track up to 50 friends simultaneously with real-time updates and performance optimization.

Background Sync

Maintain an up-to-date board with auto-refresh intervals customizable in your settings.
